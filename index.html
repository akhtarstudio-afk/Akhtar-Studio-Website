How We Work

Simple. Fast. No Surprises.

A clear four-step process every client goes through — from first call to launch day.

01

Discovery Call

A free 30-minute call. You tell us about your business, goals, and what you need. We ask the right questions — no obligations.

02

Proposal & Scope

We send a clear proposal: exact scope, timeline, and fixed price. No surprises mid-project. You approve and we start immediately.

03

Design & Build

We design in Figma first — you see exactly how it looks before a line of code is written. Then we build in WordPress with 2–3 revision rounds.

04

Launch & Handoff

We launch your site and hand over everything — files, credentials, and a walkthrough. You're in full control from day one.
